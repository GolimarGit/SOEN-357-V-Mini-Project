User Research and Persona Creation

User Research

User research is a critical step in the UX design process. It involves understanding the needs, behaviors, and pain points of the target audience.

Shared Survey

The shared survey was distributed to a diverse group of participants. It included questions about their health habits, preferences, and challenges with existing health apps.

Survey Responses

The survey received over 200 responses. Key findings include a strong preference for personalized features and a desire for better integration with wearable devices.

Persona Creation

Based on the research findings, we created three primary user personas. Each persona represents a distinct segment of our target audience and includes details about their goals, motivations, and pain points.
